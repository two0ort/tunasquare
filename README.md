# Tunasquare
##### An affordable smart chess companion
> Tunasquare is a device for you physical chess board that runs Stockfish and lets you play against bots or other people!

- Made with affordability in mind
- Plays online against real people with [Lichess](https://lichess.org)
- Also lets you play against Stockfsh

### How do you play with it, you might ask?
Simply turn on the device by plugging it into a power source, pick your mode with the on-device screen and buttons ranging from 1-8 and A-H. You enter your moves in algabraic notation (e2e4). Then, the screen displays a move in the same format, and you copy that on your physical board. That's it!
